v1.9.0

New Statistic: SOLVE COUNT! Every board you solve will now be tracked and sent to the Records page!

v1.8.1

New IMPOSSIBLE Board! 30x30 is now officially available. Only attempt if you have three to four hours of free time!

v1.8.0

New EXTREME Board! 25x25 is now officially available. Only attempt if you have a couple hours of free time!

v1.7.0

New Feature: DARK MODE! You can try it out by clicking on "Switch to Dark Mode" on any board. Dark Mode makes everything darker, which should be way easier on the eyes.

v1.6.2

Bug fixes for the Records page.

v1.6.1

Bug fixes regarding the styling of the Records page.

v1.6.0

New Feature: RECORDS PAGE! Here you can view ALL of your records from ALL of the boards! You can even reset them if you want to…

v1.5.1

Fixed a bug where when you solved the 8x10 board faster or with less moves than your record, that record was not stored properly.

v1.5.0

New Difficulties! 8x10 and 10x12 are now available!

v1.4.0

New Feature: KEYBOARD COMPATIBILITY! You can now press N on your keyboard to start a new game, and you can use the WASD keys or the arrow keys to move the blocks! Enjoy!

v1.3.1

New Feature: CHANGELOG! Here you'll find all the latest news regarding Block Puzzle and updates.

v1.3.0

You will now get a prompt that asks if you want to start a new game should you come across an unsolvable board.

v1.2.1

New Feature: SPEED! You can now see your speed in moves per second! Works for any board!

v1.2.0

New Feature: BEST TIME AND MOVES! You can now keep track of your records easier than ever!

v1.1.4

New Rectangular Boards! 3x4, 4x5, 5x6, and 6x8 are now available!

v1.1.3

New Difficulties! 20x20 is now available! Be warned… it's really big!

v1.1.2

New Difficulties! 12x12 and 15x15 are now available!

v1.1.1

New Difficulties! 6x6, 8x8, and 10x10 are now available!

v1.1.0

New Difficulties! 5x5 and 3x3 are now available!

v1.0.0

Block Puzzle is now officially released! Move the blocks with your mouse using the hole provided and rearrange the numbers to solve the 4x4 puzzle!
